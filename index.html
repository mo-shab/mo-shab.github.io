Features

Accounts Viewer

The Accounts page is your central hub for managing all your financial information at a glance. Here, you can easily view each of your accounts along with key details, including current balances, and account status if it is on hold. With a clear and organized layout, tracking your finances has never been simpler, allowing you to stay on top of your financial health with ease.

Transactions Viewer

The Transactions page is your go-to place for tracking all your financial activity. Here, you can easily view and review every transaction, whether sent or received, in a clear and organized format. With real-time updates and detailed records, staying on top of your finances has never been more convenient or transparent.

Transactcional - Swap system

The Swap system offers a seamless way to transfer funds between any accounts within the platform, including your own. This powerful feature ensures secure and hassle-free transactions by automatically verifying the sender's balance and account status before processing the swap. With its robust safeguards and user-friendly interface, the Swap system makes managing your transfers quick, reliable, and effortless.
